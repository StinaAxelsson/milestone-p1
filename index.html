About

Tommy, that is the name behind Wizack Twizack. Was born 1981 in Stockholm in Sweden.
He has been active in the Psytrance scene for over a decade now. Started in early 2000. Since his first release 2005 with the album "Behind The Trees" he have Released 6 Artist Albums, 2 own compilations, many EP's + tons of singel tracks on various compilations. Have played all around the world in great partys and festivals and have 2 other projects called Art of Existence (AOEX) (Fullon) with Nevarakka. And Circle, (Progressive)

When Tommy doesn't play at festivals, or make new songs he also is a dad of four beautiful kids and work with people at a blbl home. For now, there is not so many partys to play at because of covid, but in the same time it's more time to make more music for the future.
